About Us

We are a bunch of San Jose State University students pursuing their Masters in Software Engineering with a zeal to develop interesting innovative and useful applications. Yelp For Help started out as a class project for one of our courses in October 2015. Yelp For Help version 1.0 covers just the city of San Francisco. We are planning to take this project forward to cover the entire USA one day.
